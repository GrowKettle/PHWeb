Big Idea

A Scouting unit is an idea. An idea to develop leaders, to serve the community, to have fun, and to achieve the goals of Scouting.

That idea becomes real through meetings, outings, service projects, and adventures in the coming days, weeks, months, and years. These form the unit’s program. The program is documented through the unit calendar. And the calendar communicates the unit’s program to families, Scouts, and the community.

What’s Here

Here you'll find inspiration as you build your unit’s program, as you build on what’s already working.

Guides: a collection of the best ideas of Scouting leaders to guarantee that you can meet the goals of Scouting.

Resources: ideas and materials from across the U.S. to nurture your creativity as you build your unit.

Units: contacts for all Scouting units in Kettle Country. You aren’t alone. There are hundreds of Scouting leaders across Kettle Country who are anxious to share their experiences in delivering great Scouting experiences.
