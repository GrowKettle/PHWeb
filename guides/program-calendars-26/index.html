Building Strong Units Through Program Calendars

What if your unit’s program felt clear, predictable—and something scouts, volunteers, and families looked forward to? Nine 30-minute webinars exploring practical ways to build a stronger program through planning, communications, and calendars.

Starting in June '26, Kettle Country began 30-minute webinars to help every pack, troop, crew, and ship build a clear, predictable program based on the unit calendar. Join upcoming live webinars via Zoom. All unit leaders, committee members, and commissioners are welcome.

Whether your unit is rebuilding, already strong, or just needs a nudge in the right direction, this series will help you...

  • Reduce leader stress
  • Build stronger participation
  • Create a program scouts want to come back to

Webinar Schedule:
Past Recordings and Downloaded Materials, Upcoming Previews

See past session pages for recordings and material downloads.

  • Session 1: Tuesday, 6/23/26, 7 PM CDT - A Calendar Is a Promise

    Why a calendar is more than dates—it establishes expectations and builds trust within the unit.

  • Session 2: Tuesday, 7/7/26, 7 PM CDT - Let the Calendar Build Itself

    School and community events plus unit recurring favorites form the framework for a predictable annual program. Get these on your calendar and fill in the gaps.

  • Session 3: Tuesday, 7/21/26, 7 PM CDT - Predictability Builds Trust

    Predictable program rhythms retain families. Builk a monthly cadence on an outdoor activity baseline.

  • Session 4: Tuesday, 8/4/26, 7 PM CDT - From Random Events to Real Program

    Strong programs have intentional flow. Monthly themes and advancement integration create a cohesive program and avoide burnout.

  • Session 5: Tuesday, 8/18/26, 7 PM CDT - Stop Doing It All Yourself

    Sustainability comes from shared ownership. Reduce leader stress by delegating responsibilities and building a strong team.

  • Session 6: Tuesday, 9/1/26, 7 PM CDT - New Families Decide Fast

    The first 90 days determine retention. Develop welcome moments, a buddy system, and a clear onboarding process.

  • Session 7: Tuesday, 9/15/26, 7 PM CDT - Don’t Lose It Once It Starts

    Execution determines success or failure. Maintain weekly communications about the next events. Give a glimpse into the program's cohesive future through the unit calendar.

  • Session 8: Tuesday, 9/29/26, 7 PM CDT - What Strong Units Do Differently

    Small patterns drive long-term strength. Balance tradition and novelty. Build invitation points, reinforce strength.

  • Session 9: Tuesday, 10/13/26, 7 PM CDT - Build Next Year Before It Starts

    Strong units build forward. Expand your unit calendar to 12 or even 18 months. Develop a strategic mindset.
